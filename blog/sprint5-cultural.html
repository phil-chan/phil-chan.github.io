What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?

That I can be pretty arrogant thinking that I can just simply spend hours and hours until I find a solution, but the better way is to simply ask for help when I hit a big roadblock. Even after I find a solution on my own, I sometimes talk about it with someone else and it lets me get a much better idea of how to do things. I should do that earlier on.

What are the role of values, empathy and self awareness in learning and programming? 

You really have to be open minded in all aspects to do well and work efficiently. To connect with others you need both need to be very tolerable and understanding - they may have great advice to give you even if you don't think they can help your current project.
