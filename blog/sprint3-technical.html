Explain the difference between accessing data from arrays and objects. 

Arrays are simply a group of the same data: if you have apples, oranges, and bananas. You can store them as a fruits basket or ARRAY to be more organised - the first item in fruits array is apple, second item is oranges etc.

Objects are like arrays within arrays: instead of just having fruits, you now also have meats.
Since fruits and meats can't be put together, you have to store them in something like a fridge or a fridge OBJECT which can have mulitple sections - you have a fruits section and a meats section.
For fruits, you can refer to fruits by saying: go to the fridge and look into the fruits section, which you could find apples etc. 
For meats, you would do the same thing: go to the fridge and look into the meats section, you would could find steak etc.

Explain what functions are and why they are useful.

Functions are basically snippits of code that you can re-use, they give you more structure. 
Imagine you're running a restaurant.
You employ staff and all of their job descriptions are just "you help me run the restaurant". Things are fine until you get a lot of customers, which is when everything falls apart since you are so unorganised. 
You reorganise these staff to give them specific 'functions' like Kitchen, Table and Admin staff. You now have an organised structure which can handle catering to a lot of customers. 
You can even employ casual staff to help during those busy times without having to micro-manage them since you can place them into specific functions.
